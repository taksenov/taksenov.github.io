How Does Heart In Diamond Create Beautiful Diamonds from Your Loved One’s Hair or Ashes?
We follow a simple, 5-step process to turn human ashes into diamonds that hold lasting memories of your loved one:
  1. Select your memorial diamond
  2. Select your setting (over 500 to choose from)
  3. Send your loved one’s (family member’s, relative’s, friend’s, or pet’s) hair or ashes
  4. Our lab turns their ashes into diamonds
  5. Receive your personal diamond

Our kind and compassionate consultants guide you hand-in-hand through the process. They’ll stay in constant contact with you. You’ll feel special, loved, and valued.

Step 1: Select Your «Conflict-Free» Memorial Diamond without Any Guilt or Worry

First, you’ll choose your diamond with the help of our sensitive, thoughtful consultants. All cremation diamonds are laboratory grown over a period of just months in our own production facility.

Our laboratory-made diamonds, unlike natural diamonds, do not cause any harm to Earth’s environment (Digging deep into Earth for diamonds destroys the environment so much local populations sometimes have to relocate).

And laboratory diamonds are guaranteed 100% «conflict-free» because they’re made by experienced professionals. Natural diamonds are often harvested by slaves — even children — and the profits from them can be used to fund wars and terrorism.

Regardless of which diamond you choose to commemorate your loved one, you buy it with a totally clear conscience. If possible, our consultants will even come to visit you in person.
